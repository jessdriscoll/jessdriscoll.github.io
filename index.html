You can call me Jess or Jessica; my pronouns are she/her and singular they. I live on the unceded territory of the Semiahmoo Nation. As part of my commitment to unsettle Turtle Island, I redistribute 10% of my income to support Indigenous children and families.

Since 2002, I've written a trail of blogs across the internet (the first on Blogspot) and a ten-year back catalogue of zines. My first published book of nonfiction will be out in 2022 from Microcosm Publishing.
